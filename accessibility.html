Accessibility

Access and platform support.

Accessibility and device support expectations for the 20Realms website, game, and platform releases.

Platform review

BloodStone Forge should review platform accessibility, safety, and reporting expectations before submission to Apple, Google, Steam, console storefronts, or other platforms.

Accurate feature claims

Store pages and in-game notices should avoid misleading claims about accessibility, device support, multiplayer behavior, Creator Lab availability, marketplace behavior, or platform-specific limitations.

Device recommendations

Anyone may Dungeon Master from supported devices where available. BloodStone Forge recommends Dungeon Master use on PC, console, or tablet. If a user chooses to Dungeon Master from a mobile device, 2D mode is recommended until mobile devices and mobile builds can fully support heavier Dungeon Master workflows.

Preferences and cookie choices

Functional cookies and local preferences may remember language, UI settings, accessibility settings, cookie choices, and similar selections. Blocking some cookies or local storage may affect site or game functionality.

Contact

For accessibility concerns or support needs, contact support@20realms.net.
